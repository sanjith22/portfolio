02 — Problem

Fragmented internal workflows cost time and equipment.

Before Dronos, bookings were tracked via spreadsheets and chat — causing scheduling conflicts, unclear availability, and no audit trail for handoffs.

No real-time visibility

Operators couldn't see which drones were available without calling the warehouse.

Manual approval chain

Multi-level approvals happened over email with no centralized tracking.

Handoff gaps

No verification process for pickup/return — accountability issues for missing assets.

Schedule conflicts

Double-bookings were common for high-demand equipment like Matrice 300.

09 — Takeaways

What shaped the design.

1

8-step approval mirrors Aerodyne's ops

Multi-level authorization matches real drone handoff protocols.

2

QR eliminates paper handoffs

Digital audit trail for every checkout and return.

3

Bundles reduce booking errors

Recommended payloads/batteries prevent missing accessories.

4

Gantt prevents conflicts

Visual calendar eliminates double-bookings for high-demand gear.
